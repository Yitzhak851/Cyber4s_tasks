    Childhood and education

    Yitzhak grew up and was educated in Ashkelon.
    Compulsory kindergarten, through elementary schools, to middle school.
    In the upper division, he decided to study at a military boarding school in Haifa.

    Military career

    In November 2015, he was drafted into the Paratroopers, a battalion called No. 890.
    Underwent 8-month combat training, captured a line in Hebron, and took a cadet course.
    He then commanded 4 months of recruits at the paratroopers' brigade training base.
    Went to an officers course.
    Commanded recruits for 8 months,
    after which we became a team leader of a team that fights with missiles called "Gil" and observations.
    4 months in the Gaza line and 4 months of training.
    He was the deputy commander of a company, and an AGM officer on the Nablus line.
